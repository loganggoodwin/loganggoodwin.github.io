How I work

I approach technical work with structure, security, and supportability in mind.

Start with business and risk context

I like understanding what a system supports, where the operational risk sits, and what the impact of failure would be before making design or support decisions.

Design for least privilege and defense in depth

I think reliable environments come from layered security, controlled access, and infrastructure choices that reduce exposure while staying practical for day-to-day operations.

Document for handoff and auditability

I believe strong technical work should be easy to follow, easier to support, and clear enough for teammates, leadership, or auditors to understand the reasoning behind decisions.
