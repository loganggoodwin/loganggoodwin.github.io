Overview

This operations plan defines how an organization supports a major cloud implementation after go-live—people, process, governance, and continuity. I like ops planning because it reduces failure modes before they happen.

What This Covers

  • Strategic alignment and mission support
  • Business continuity: RPO/RTO, testing, and communications
  • Vendor management: due diligence and performance scorecards
  • Compliance standards and audit readiness
  • SLAs/NDAs and contractual governance

Skills Demonstrated

  • IT operations planning and service management thinking
  • Risk-aware continuity and vendor governance
  • Clear documentation and operational discipline

What I Would Improve Next

  • Add KPIs/SLAs dashboard metrics
  • Create a RACI matrix and escalation map
  • Add tabletop exercises for major incidents
